AEG International Style

Tasked with the challenge of redesigning an Art Deco poster, this reimagined AEG poster emulates the clean, universal feels of the International Style. When the world was stuck by devastating world wars, design called for a universal approach. Design during the mid 1900’s favored sans-serif fonts and minimal type treatments in an attempt to remove nationalistic styling. We can also observe this Swiss-lead International Style having used highly saturated, primary colors.

Scanography Seashells

What is Scanography? As applied in this image, Scanography is the use of a digital scanner to capture an object at various levels of detail. In playing with the scanner’s dpi, both recognizable and unrecognizable details were captured from the seashells used. By arranging this composition in layers, the technique of “scratching through” or erasing is used to create a unique sense of depth.

Four of Wands

In this iteration of the Four of Wands, I drew inspiration from my personal life. This tarot card can relate to things of home. The clematis plant along with other flowers are staples of my childhood. Thus, the clematis is representing my home. The four wands are incorporated as the lattice that holds the clematis plant up.
