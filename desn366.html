Rummy Mummy

Hover / click images for additional information.

Pitch Take the card game Rummy and introduce the Mummy. This wild card will add surprise and haste to this classic card game.
Tagline The game you love with a twist.
The Project Design a game. This can either be a redesign of an existing and or / a new game altogether.
The Solution Using the classic card game Rummy as a basis, Rummy Mummy is a redesign of playing cards and the addition of a wild card.
The Red Mummy A Red Mummy can act as any card for your hand.
The Black Mummy A Black Mummy can replace / steal any card that has been played.
Analytical Cubism This art style is characterized by its geometric, abstract forms.
Art Deco This architectural and design style is very geometric, decadent, and bold.
Constructivism This art, political, and design style can be simple, bold, geometric, and full of tension.
Tattoos Just like art, tattoos can be drawn in different styles. The sketch / line qualities stood out for inspiration.
Zine-a-Day Every day for a week, a Zine – comprising of sketches – was made for a different topic.
The Cards Bold and abstract ideas were contemplated for representing the traditional, playing cards.
Experimenting After experimenting with different line qualities, this line-quality direction did not work.
Simplifying Contemplating alternative design aesthetics, a bold and simple approach was chosen.
Specs Typeface: BC Sklonar. Black: 60 40 40 100. Maroon: 34 98 96 52. White: 0 0 0 0. Cards 2.5” x 3.5”. Box 2.5” x 3.66” x .7”.
The King The Kings is depicted as a wide rectangle to denote his strong, masculine character.
The Queen The Queen is depicted as a triangular hourglass to denote her beautiful, feminine character.
The Jack The Jack is depicted as a tall, thin rectangle to denote his youthful, masculine character.
The Mummy The Mummy is depicted similar to the beautiful, feminine characteristics of the Queen, but she is a little more robust.
The Joker In this context, the double dagger represents the double-edged nature of a Jester’s job.
The Badge To denote which face card belongs to which suite, a badge is worn.
Figure and Ground Each card utilizes a strong use of figure-ground.
The Box After considering personal, local, and online print option, this project was not printed.
Final Thoughts I am very happy with the outcome of this project. I feel that the design is strong and compelling.
