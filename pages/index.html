If it can be printed
it can be designed

Tannor Glumbik specializes in designing print marketing. He uses his Bachelor of Design and Business Administration minor from Eastern Washington University – to be completed in Spring of 2021 – to advertise your business, product, or service.
In addition to his design skills, Tannor Glumbik excels in leadership and teaching. He is seeking an MFA to further immerse himself in graphic design and pedagogy. A career goal for Tannor is teaching graphic design at a college institution.
