Meet the Designer

Tannor Glumbik is a junior at Eastern Washington University where he is gaining both knowledge and hands on experience with his Bachelor of Design major. Tannor is also pursuing a minor in Business Administration: a minor that prepares students to succeed in areas of management and to oversee various business departments.

In this class I furthered my knowledge in sound design by studying and practicing track mixing, foley sound effects, voice recording, etc. For me, this class was special for it furthered an already existing interest in sound design.
